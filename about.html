My Education Journey

Education has always been the foundation of my growth. I began my schooling at Brook Field School in North Lakhimpur, where I studied from Nursery to Grade 4 between 2015 and 2021. Those years were crucial in shaping my curiosity and discipline. Later, I moved to LK Singhania Education Centre in Gotan for Grades 5 and 6, where I was introduced to new subjects and extracurricular activities that broadened my perspective. Since 2024, I have been studying at GD Goenka International School in Surat, where I am currently in Grade 8. This school has given me opportunities to explore technology more deeply, participate in projects, and collaborate with peers. Each institution has played a unique role in my journey, helping me grow academically and personally. I believe that education is not just about memorizing facts but about learning how to think, solve problems, and innovate. My academic journey reflects my adaptability, resilience, and eagerness to embrace challenges. I am proud of the progress I have made and excited about the future opportunities that lie ahead.
