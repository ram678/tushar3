About Me

My name is Tushar Malpani, and I am currently a Grade 8 student at GD Goenka International School, Surat. Over the years, I have developed a strong interest in technology, website development, and automation tools. My journey began at Brook Field School in North Lakhimpur, where I first discovered my love for learning and curiosity about how things work. Later, at LK Singhania Education Centre in Gotan, I started experimenting with small projects, which gave me confidence to explore more advanced ideas. Today, I am actively building websites, experimenting with automation, and learning new technologies that can make life easier and more efficient. I believe that education is not just about grades but about discovering passions and applying knowledge in creative ways. This website is my personal portfolio where I share my educational journey, skills, projects, and memorable experiences. My vision is to continue learning, improving, and inspiring others to explore the exciting world of technology. I want this platform to reflect my growth, creativity, and dedication to building something meaningful. Every section here is written in detail to give you a complete picture of who I am and what I aim to achieve.
